Garlic Noodles



Description:

Garlic noodles tossed in a rich, umami-packed sauce made with soy, oyster, Worcestershire, and sesame.
Butter-sautéed garlic is combined with the sauce, then spaghetti, pasta water, and Parmesan are tossed in.
Finished with green onions and red pepper flakes for a quick, flavorful weeknight dish.


Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, Worcestershire sauce, fish sauce, sesame oil, and cayenne.
  2. Melt butter, sauté garlic until fragrant, then stir in the sauce and turn off heat.
  3. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  4. Toss pasta with the sauce, adding pasta water and Parmesan as needed.
  5. Garnish with green onions and red pepper flakes.
