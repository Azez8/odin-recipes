No-Rise Pizza Dough



Description:

This no-rise pizza dough is quick and easy to make, requiring just a few basic ingredients and no waiting time.
Simply mix, shape, and bake for a soft, delicious pizza crust that’s perfect for busy days.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 package (0.25 oz) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water (110°F / 45°C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add warm water and vegetable oil; mix until a dough forms.
  3. Press and stretch the dough onto a pizza pan.
  4. Add sauce and desired toppings.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20–25 minutes until lightly browned.
