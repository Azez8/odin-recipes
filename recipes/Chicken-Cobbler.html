Chicken Cobbler



Description:

This viral TikTok chicken cobbler is a comforting, one-pan dish made with shredded chicken, frozen vegetables, and a creamy filling, all topped with buttery Cheddar Bay biscuit mix.
It’s incredibly easy to prepare and perfect for busy weeknights or feeding a crowd.


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 (15 oz) bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 package Cheddar Bay biscuit mix (with seasoning packet)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and melt butter in a 9×13-inch baking dish.
  2. Layer chicken over butter, then add frozen vegetables evenly.
  3. Mix biscuit mix, seasoning, and milk; pour over chicken and veggies (do not stir).
  4. Whisk soup and stock; pour over the top without stirring.
  5. Bake uncovered for about 45 minutes until golden brown; rest 10 minutes, garnish, and serve.
